🌹 A Love Letter for Rose 🌹

My dearest Rose, this is not just a letter… it’s a little journey. Where shall we begin?

I remember the first time I saw your smile… it felt like the world paused.

I promise you this: no matter the storms, I’ll choose you, again and again.

What I felt was warmth, like home finally found in human form. And I knew from then on—you’d be my favorite story.

I knew right away… you were someone I’d never want to lose. A kind of knowing that settles deep, like destiny whispering your name.

My vow of the heart: to love you softly when you’re tired, to love you loudly when you forget your worth, to love you always—without condition.

My vow of the future: to walk beside you through ordinary days, to build a life where our laughter echoes, to write years with you like chapters in our forever book.

🌹 My Rose…

At the end of every path, every choice, every story— it all leads back to one truth:
You are my always. 💖
